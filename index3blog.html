How to Style a Quiff

A waistcoat, also known as a vest, is a sleeveless garment typically worn over a shirt and under a suit jacket. It adds a touch of sophistication to formal and semi-formal attire, often featuring buttons down the front and sometimes pockets. Traditionally part of a three-piece suit, waistcoats can also be styled casually for a modern, layered look. They come in various fabrics, colors, and patterns, making them a versatile addition to any wardrobe.

AW20 Menswear Trends

The AW20 menswear trends showcased a blend of classic tailoring and contemporary streetwear influences. Oversized coats, layered textures, and statement knitwear dominated the runways, emphasizing warmth and style. Earthy tones, rich neutrals, and deep jewel colors set the seasonal palette, while leather and wool played key roles in outerwear. Utility-inspired pieces, such as cargo pants and workwear jackets, added a functional yet stylish edge.
